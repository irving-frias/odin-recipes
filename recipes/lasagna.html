Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound sweet Italian sausage
  • 3/4 pound anchovy fillets
  • 1/4 cup minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons white wine
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 1/2 cups pasta
  • 1/2 pound shredded Mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 6 cups ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. In a large skillet over medium-high heat, brown sausage, anchovies, and garlic.
  3. Stir in sugar, wine, oregano, and basil.
  4. Bring to a simmer over medium heat, stirring occasionally, until mixture is thick and bubbly, about 10 minutes.
  5. Remove from heat.
  6. Spread sauce evenly over lasagna.
  7. Layer with remaining sauce, noodles, cheese, and remaining ingredients.
  8. Cover with lasagna noodles.
  9. Bake until bubbly and cheese is melted, about 30 minutes.
