Cheesecake

Description

Cheesecake is a rich and creamy dessert made with a mixture of cream cheese, sugar, and eggs, usually on top of a graham cracker or pastry crust. It can be flavored with various ingredients like vanilla, lemon, chocolate, or fruit and is often topped with fresh fruit, whipped cream, or fruit sauces.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 4 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese, softened
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 1/4 cup heavy cream

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 325 degrees F (165 degrees C).
  2. In a medium bowl, mix graham cracker crumbs, melted butter, and 2 tablespoons sugar until well combined. Press the mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
  3. In a large bowl, beat cream cheese and 1 cup sugar until smooth. Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition.
  4. Stir in vanilla extract, sour cream, and heavy cream until fully incorporated.
  5. Pour the cream cheese mixture over the prepared crust.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for about 55 minutes, or until the center is set but still slightly jiggly.
  7. Turn off the oven and allow the cheesecake to cool inside with the door slightly open for 1 hour.
  8. Refrigerate for at least 4 hours before serving.
  9. Top with your choice of fresh fruit, whipped cream, or fruit sauce before serving.
